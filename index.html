Welcome to Our School

At God's Eminent Kids International School Our curriculum is tailored towards the significance of our colour. We provide an enduring education that brings about self-reliability in children and gives the parents happiness even at old age.


Students are engaged daily using a blend of British and Nigerian Curriculum, having a broad number of subject areas. They interact with great books and materials. They are encouraged to love their studies and to find delight in Godly and moral values. Through training in habits of mind and attention, God's Eminent students are equipped with the tools they need for a lifetime of learning and service.
